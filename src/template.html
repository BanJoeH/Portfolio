About me

All my life I have been attracted to problem solving. This is evident in two of my favourite things, Rock climbing and Carpentry. At their core they are just solving problems, whether that is how to take a set of plans and make them into a beautiful piece of furniture or finding the best way up a piece of rock in Fontainbleau, France. This is what attracted me to a career in development. And I'm loving it.

Being on furlough in 2020 gave me the opportunity to work out exactly what I wanted to do and when I tried web development, I was hooked. I started the course "Zero to mastery, The complete web developer 2020/21" which taught me how to think as a developer and made me hungry to learn more. View CV
